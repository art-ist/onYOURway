    Needs

    • provides: food
      Here you can find food, that is produced sustainably and cooperatively or 'saved' from being thrown away. Some is shared, some produced together, and some is given away.
    • provides: beverages
      Here you can find water and other drinks, free for everyone or produced and distributed in a cooperative way
    • provides: land
      Here you find land to use and share, which has been withdrawn from the real estate market.
    • provides: mobility
      Here you find possibilities to go from A to B without having to pay the usual market price. By joining someone in his/her car, driving together or sharing a car, to name a few
    • provides: health
      Here you find what keeps and makes us healthy - without health insurance. Healthcare and preventive health are organised collectively
    • provides: culture
      Here you find non-commercial cultural events or free spaces to organize an event or to perform as an artist yourself. Or you can join facilities/events without a ticket and for a donation.
    • provides: safety
      Places and people that provide a sense of security, support and safety.
    • provides: housing
      Here you find shelter - for a short time or permanently without buying or renting it from the real estate market.
    • provides: space
      Here you find free spaces to meet, hang out or do whatever you want.
    • provides: objets_of_utility
      Here you can get or borrow different tools and other things, that others don't need any longer or only ocassionally use.
    • provides: clothes
      Here you can find clothes that are secondhand, homemade, produced collectively or come from fair-trade sources.
    • provides: knowledge
      Here is a space where knowledge is shared or where people learn and research together.
    • provides: skills
      Here you can learn practical, useful knowledge, that engages head and hand.
    • provides: energy
      Electricity, heating, transportation - all these require energy. Here you find projects, ideas and places, where this energy is generated sustainable or collectively.
    • provides: community
      A diverse group of people that offers support and empowerment by coming together.
    • provides: participation
      Here is an opportunity to initiate activities, participate or deliberate on public issues.

    Mode of Production

    • P2P
      Here you find things produced P2P, that is in a cooperative way among peers, where everybody contributes what he/she can. See also: http://p2pfoundation.net/Peer_Production
    • diy&dit
      Here you can produce or repair, upcycle or downcycle something by yourself or together with others.
    • Commoning
      Here, commons are produced, that is people produce and use together what they need for a good and meaningful life.
    • Open Manufacturing
      Here the process of designing, producing and distributing physical goods is defined by combining open knowledge with distributed infrastructures.
    • fair-production
      Projects, cooperatives and firms who cooperates with others to fullfill the needs of all people involved and to contribute to a locally embedded, sustainable and globally fair economy.
